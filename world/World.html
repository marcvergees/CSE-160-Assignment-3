Instructions

Welcome, Traveler, to the heart of the realm. Master these arts to navigate the fortress:

  • W, A, S, D: Traverse the grounds (Forward, Left, Back, Right)
  • Q, E or Arrows: Gaze upon your surroundings
  • Space: Leap into the air
  • Double Space: A grander leap (Double Jump)
  • Mouse Drag: Command your vision
  • P: Return to the beginning (Reset Camera)
  • ESC: Hasten your arrival (Skip Intro)

May your journey be legendary.

The Peril

Within these stone walls, you are not alone. A vengeful ghost stalks the corridors, seeking to claim your soul. If the specter draws near, your life will be extinguished in an instant.

You must navigate the dark corners of the castle and uncover its secrets, all while staying one step ahead of your spectral hunter.

Tread carefully: the fortress borders the abyss. If you fail to stay within the walls and fall from the world, you will perish immediately.

Survive the hunt. Escape the fortress.
